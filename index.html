What we do

Previously known as us.youth, generation we started out with the mission of empowering youths. However, we have shifted our mission and vision to a more specific segment within empowerment: mental health and advoacy. Gen we. is for youths in Malaysia who want to speak out about a social injustice, we are the bridge to other resources. Unlike what is available on the web, the information we provide is tailored and local. Our emphasis on mental health is something that is not seen in the youth changemakers community. We will curate a stand up culture in Malaysia so we know that we are all in this together.

Voix

Voix magazine aims to make more advocates out of Generation Z as we live in an age in which it is vital for our voices to be heard. Through the magazine, we raise awareness on issues under mental health and advocacy which is what Generation We as an organization stands for. Through information, we hope that those who pride themselves under Generation Z, see to it that we make society a better place for us all.

GW Makers

At Generation We, we understand the importance of a role model, someone to look up to and to reach out to if a situation ever arises. This is why, we started Gen Makers. A club with changemakers around Asia whom have volunteered to be reached out to by anyone. If you are interested in environmental activism but don't know where to start, simply reach out!

Change 101

Starting is always the hardest. We are here to help. We have partnered with various non-profit organisations to develop toolkits that explain the basics of a certain area within social change ( eg. climate change ) and linked all the resources as well as step by step guide on getting started. Reach out to our GW Makers if you need further guidance! Taking initiative shouldn't be hard, let's make it easy.

Fund The Period

Fund The Period raised a total of RM4650 to purchase 300 reusable pads for 100 girls in the Rohingya Community. We partnered with Small Changes and are sponsored by Athena Empowers. To raise the amount needed, we sold digitally drawn portraits, stickers and relied on donations. Besides just donating pads, we will soon be hosting workshops in order to discuss period stigma as well as how to use reusable pads.

Past project: Need to know

We have covered topics ranging from Black Lives Matter to Medusa where we shine a light on topics that we deem important, that we believe that youths in Malaysia needs to know about.

Past project: Pride Month

Sometimes it is enough just knowing that you are not alone. Through our pride month project, we attempted to start conversations as well as show that we care, that you are not alone. We interviewed members of the LGBTQ+ community from Malaysia and asked them to share their stories. We also created posts with the aim of education as a lot of people are still in the dark due to this topic being a taboo
